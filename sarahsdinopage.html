Can computers help us learn more about dinosaurs?

fossils

According to the BBC, scientists at the Natural History Musuem have used 3D modelling to help them identify which dinosaur teeth belong to, using AI to help create these models by feeding fossil information to it.

This is a very positive innovation as it has allowed the discovery of the first therizinosaur fossil.
